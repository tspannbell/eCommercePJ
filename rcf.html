Product Description:

The careful acoustic design, no compromise transducers and a unique composite cabinet design make the HDL 10-A a preferred tool of many professionals. It is perfect in both mobile live sound reinforcement and in installations. The HDL 10-A is a fine choice when line array performance is needed and a fast and easy set up is a must. The system features two powerful state-of-the-art 8 in. RCF transducers with 2 in. voice coils for a solid bass reproduction, and a large-format 2 in. compression driver with 3 in. voice coil to deliver vocal clarity and high definition. The large format compression driver RCF CD 850 is a key advantage of the HDL10-A. All the vocal range is reproduced from this driver. The system is powered by a 700-Watt Digital Amplifier that packs huge performance with high efficiency into a lightweight solution. HDL10-A amplifiers presents SMPS power supply section in order to produce maximum output and minimum transportation weight.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Active/passive: Active
  • Type: Full range
  • LF driver: 2 x 8 in. w/2 in. VC
