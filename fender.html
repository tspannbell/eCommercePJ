Product Description:

The Fender Passport VENUE is a self-contained portable audio system that includes everything you need for great sound anywhere you go. Carry your Passport as you would a suitcase, and simply flip open the cabinet release latches when you're ready to set it up. Inside you'll discover two full-range speaker cabinets, a powered mixer and all the cables you'll need to get started. Passport VENUE is ideal for an array of applications, including band and DJ gigs; education, sporting and worship events; meetings, seminars and presentations. Passport VENUE also features a convenient USB record/playback system that lets you record WAV audio files using a USB flash drive and play WAV and MP3 files.

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Active/passive: Active
  • Type: Full range
  • LF driver: 2 x 8 in. w/2 in. VC
  • MF driver: N/A
  • HF driver: 2 x 1.5 in., 3 in. VC Power
  • Amplifier class: D
  • Wattage: 700W RMS 1400 peak
  • Mixer/preamp: N/A
