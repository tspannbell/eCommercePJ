Product Description:

Harbinger M120 MV Portable PA system is a simple solution for anyone needing a simple, portable and affordable sound system for smaller performances, presentations and rehearsals. Designed for more intimate spaces, the Harbinger M120 system includes a 4-channel powered mixer, loudspeakers and even speaker cables. You also get four microphone/instrument inputs with separate volume controls, 3-band Master EQ, plus MP3/Aux input for music or backing track playback. Built-in 1-3/8" speaker stand mounts allow you to add your own stands for additional versatility. It's an ideal system for singer/songwriters, presenters, DJs and instructors who need to stay mobile.
